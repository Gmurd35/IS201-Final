Gameplay

The game is played with a guitar shaped controller that has a strum bar and 5 buttons colored green, red, yellow, blue, and orange. Your objective is to play the notes that scroll on screen by pressing the appropriate color and strumming simultaneously, allowing you to make it through each rock song.

The campaign has 39 songs that you must beat. These songs are split up into 8 sections with sections 1-7 having 5 songs and section 8 having 4 songs. In order to move on to the next section you must beat every song in the previous section. Songs become more difficult as you move on to later sections.

The game also has 3 bosses that you must defeat. These bosses are legendary guitarists Tom Morello, Slash, and Lou. Tom Morello is the guitarist for Rage Against the Machine, Slash is Guns N Roses guitarist, and Lou is a demon like goat who can absolutely shred.

After defeating all 3 of these bosses you unlock your final challenge. The song Through the Fire and Flames. This is a 7 minute song with ridiculously fast solos and strumming so fast it will make you hands cramp. Not many have been able to beat this song but if you do, you can safely call yourself a Guiter Hero God.
