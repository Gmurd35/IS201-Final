Guitar Hero III

Guitar Hero is a series of rhythm video games created by Activison and first released in November 2005, in which players use a guitar-shaped game controller to simulate playing guitar across numerous songs. Players match notes that scroll on-screen to colored fret buttons on the controller, strumming the controller in time to the music in order to score points, and keep the virtual audience excited.

Guitar Hero III is the third installment in the Guitar Hero series. The game was released on October 27th, 2007 on the Wii, Xbox 360, and the PlayStation 2 and 3.

Guitar Hero III is by far the most successful game in the series. Sales for the game surpassed 2 billion dollars, and was the first game to ever eclipse 1 billion dollars in sales.
